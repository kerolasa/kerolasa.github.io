Twinings - Forest Fruits Black

2020-10-31. Black tea. Really nice smell. Taste gives a little hint of berries. I like this one. 5

Twinings - Lapsang Souchong

2020-10-31. Smoky black tea. Great smell. Fairly straightforward smoke aroma. To be honest; I wouldn't mind this being more smoky. 4

Twinings - London Strand Strong Breakfast

2020-10-31. Black tea. Good tea without faults. Taste is better when not boiling hot. Similar to Darlington I had earlier, but not as expensive. 3

Twinings - Nutty Chocolate Assam

2020-10-31. Blak tea. Smell has nice hazelnut aroma, but I don't like test of it. In wine terms this tea is too dry. I guess this one would be better with milk, but that is not how I drink my tea. 2

Mariage Frères - Robe Rouge Suprême

2020-10-10. Blue tea. Taste give clear seaweed aroma. Good one, but this does require question is this worth the price? Maybe once, but I doubt twice. 4

Mariage Frères - Darlington

2020-10-10. Black tea. Really good smell. Not super interesting, but nothing to complain. 3

The Tea House - Rooibos Cinnamon And Vanilla

2020-09-26. Rooibos. Basically an attempt to try rooibos first time long time. Not very interesting, but better than supermarket teabags. 1

TWG Tea - Jade Dragon

2020-09-05. Green tea. I really enjoyed this one. Done again without scale, but even with just eyeballing cups turned out great. Strong recommendation. 5

Mariage Frères - Lung Ching

2020-08-22. Green tea. Nice grass aromas. I messed up when making. I did not have scale at the time, so half of the cups resulted too weak. Good one. 3
